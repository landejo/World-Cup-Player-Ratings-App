# How to use Match Ratings

A quick guide for rating FIFA World Cup matches.

**Open it here →** https://jasonlande.com/World-Cup-Player-Ratings-App/
(works on your computer or phone — nothing to install)

---

## 1. Rate a match

1. Get a match's **Post-Match Summary Report (PMSR)** PDF from FIFA's hub:
   **https://www.fifatrainingcentre.com/en/fifa-world-cup-2026/match-report-hub.php**
   (reports appear there once a match finishes — download the PDF for the game you want).
2. On the app, **drag that PDF onto the page** — or click **"click to browse"** and pick it.
3. Give it a few seconds. Every player gets rated out of 10, both teams, automatically.

> In a hurry? Click one of the **sample** buttons (Brazil–Morocco, etc.) to see it work instantly.

The PDF is read **entirely in your browser** — it's never uploaded anywhere.

---

## 2. Read the results

- **Scoreboard** up top: the score and each team's overall rating (/100).
- **Player of the Match** is starred.
- **Player cards** are colour-coded — cyan = elite, green = strong, yellow = average, orange/red = poor.
- **Sort** by Rating, Position, or Minutes (top of the list).
- **Tap any card** to expand it: a plain-English note ("2 goals, 5 line breaks…") plus the full stat grid.

## 3. Tweak a player (optional)

The model reads positions and minutes off the team sheet, but you can correct them:

- Tap a card to expand it, then change **Pos** (DF/MF/FW) or **Min**.
- The rating and team totals **update live**. (Goalkeepers stay goalkeepers.)

## 4. Save & share

Click **Export** (top right) for:

- **Save as image** — a shareable picture of the full match ratings.
- **Save data (.json)** — a backup of the match, including any edits. **Open .json** loads it back.

## 5. Build a tournament

Every match you rate is **remembered**, so they stack up. Switch the **Match / Tournament** toggle (top) to see:

- **Team of the Tournament** — the best XI across all your matches.
- **Top Performances** — the highest single-match ratings.
- **Player Leaderboard** — best average ratings (once a player appears in 2+ of your matches).

Add games as the tournament goes on — just download each new PMSR and drop it in.

---

## Good to know

- **It saves in your browser.** Your rated matches live on the device you used — they won't appear on someone else's screen. (Use **Export** to share a specific match.)
- **Parsing takes a few seconds** per match — the report PDFs are big. The spinner means it's working.
- **How the rating works:** each player earns credit for weighted on-the-ball and off-the-ball actions (passing, line breaks, take-ons, duels, pressures, goalkeeping), by position. Tap **"How it works"** in the app for the full breakdown.
